SGD Events Happening Soon!

Join SGD as we kick of the new semester with our combined Interest and Pitch Meetings on Monday, January 26th, in Thornton E-316! The Interest Meeting will take place at 6:30. New members can learn the details about the club and ask any questions they may have. The Pitch Meeting will follow directly afterwards, at 7:00. Directors will pitch their game ideas and members can select which game they would like to work on. Hope to see you all there!
